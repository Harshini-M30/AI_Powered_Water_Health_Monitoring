AI-Powered Water Health Monitoring

Real-time water quality insights using AI and IoT sensors

Explore Features

Our AI-Powered Water Health Monitoring System is a smart, portable solution aimed at ensuring clean and safe water access. By integrating IoT sensors and AI image classification, it detects water pollution in real time and explains potential health risks through an interactive chatbot. The system architecture connects hardware sensors with a Flask backend, an AI model, and a Firebase-powered dashboard for live monitoring. Designed to be affordable and scalable, it empowers rural communities, schools, and mobile labs with actionable insights rather than just raw data. This innovative fusion of AI, IoT, and health awareness aims to raise public consciousness and potentially save lives.
